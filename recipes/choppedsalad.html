Southwest Chopped Salad

Description

The chopped salad is a personal favorite of mine because of its simplicity, nutrition, and my love for Sam's Club's southwest chopped salad kit. This is such an easy, healthy meal and it pairs so well with other dishes such as grilled chicken. In this recipe, I will show you how to make a Southwest chopped chicken salad to satisfy your hunger in a delicious and budget friendly way.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 head of romain lettuce, shredded
  • 1 can sweet corn
  • 1/2 cup black beans
  • 1 Avocado, chopped
  • Handful of tortilla chips, crushed
  • Ranch dressing to taste
  • Salsa to taste
  • Pico de gallo
    • 2 tomatoes, seeded and chopped
    • 1/4 small white onion, minced
    • 1/2 Jalapeno, seeded and minced
    • 1/4 cup cilantro, chopped
    • Juice of 1/2 lime
    • Salt

Steps

  1. Combine pico de gallo ingredients
  2. Stir in 1 part ranch dressing, another part salsa
  3. Combine all other ingredients and toss
  4. Enjoy!
